Your Wilderness Board

Wilderness Trails Board Members

Board members are elected by the community at the annual meeting. All board positions are volunteer. Any subdivision lot owner is eligible.

Per the bylaws, the primary purpose of the Board is to take care of our roads. Annual assessments are collected to pay for road maintenance, including fixing damage, sealing or resufacing.

We manage contracts for trash/recycling, mowing around common areas, and winter snow removal/salting. These costs are distributed to all homeowners.

The Board also exists to serve the homeowners. We organize the Spring and Fall neighborhood work day, and the bonfire & hayride around Halloween.

Please feel free to contact us at any time at WildernessTrailsHOA@gmail.com
